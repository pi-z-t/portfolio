About

I am always “on the road” — a small nod to the restless creative spirit of Jack Kerouac and his novel On the Road. Movement, structure and story are the core of how I think about digital creation.

I worked as a technical animator on Arma 3 and as an animator on Street Power Football. I also contributed in many smaller projects as game designer, programmer, idea maker, animator and even translator.

I love games and interactive media. I have more than 10 years of experience working with Unreal Engine, focusing on precise craft, clean motion, and technically reliable animation systems.

Currently I work as freelance and am spending most of my free time on personal projects and learning new skills in Unreal Engine, Blender, and other tools. I am also open to new opportunities in the games industry and beyond.

I consider myself passionate, detail-oriented and quick to adapt. I value clarity of intention, efficient workflow, and continuous learning across technical and artistic domains.

I am a huge fan of Arsenal Football Club, love road cycling and music. I regularly play guitar and I am occasionally composing music in my small humble DAW setup.

If you want to know more about me, my work, or just want to say hi, feel free to contact me!
